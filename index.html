PUNCH THINGS!

Punch Bomb is an action arcade VR game where you punch things designed from the ground up for those who like a challenge!

This game IS NOT EASY

Rack up huge combos, hit targets, and survive in Classic Mode,

test your skills in the allotted time in Blitz Mode,

or try to conquer a collection of challenges that put your speed and accuracy to the test in Challenge Mode!

3 Punchable Game Modes!

Classic Mode

Punch bombs away before they explode, rack up massive combos, dodge dangerous obstacles, and hit targets!

Blitz Mode

Go from 0 to 100 INSTANTLY - Bombs no longer explode. Rack up a high score in the allotted time!

Challenge Mode

A fun collection of challenges to put your speed and accuracy to the test!

Collect Things!

Complete challenging milestones to unlock unique cosmetic fists - each with their own particle effects!
