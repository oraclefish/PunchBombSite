3 Punchable Game Modes!

Classic Mode

Punch bombs away before they explode, rack up massive combos, dodge dangerous obstacles, and hit targets!

Blitz Mode

Bombs no longer explode. This mode goes from 0 to 100 INSTANTLY - get as high of a score as you can in the allotted time!

Challenge Mode

A fun collection of challenges to put your speed and accuracy to the test!
